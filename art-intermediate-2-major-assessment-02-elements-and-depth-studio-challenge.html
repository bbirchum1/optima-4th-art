Elements and Depth Studio Challenge
Weeks 5-8 · Color, value, texture, space, and process

What this checks

This assessment asks you to use skills from several lessons together. It is not a copy of one lesson.

What you turn in

Typed answers, a clear art photo, and a short artist statement in Canvas.

Best effort

Plan first. Make carefully. Improve one part before you submit.

🎨 Studio Challenge
Make a new work that shows several skills together.
35 min

Your challenge: Create a layered mini-landscape or room scene that shows depth. Use color, light/dark value, and texture marks to help some parts feel near and some parts feel far.

Plan first

Make a tiny sketch or list before the final version.

Use the source wisely

Borrow an idea. Do not copy the image.

Show the skill

Make the assessment focus easy to see.

Plan: What will you make? What will the viewer notice first?
2
✅ Criteria Checklist
Use these while you work.
5 min
✓ Show foreground, middle ground, and background or another clear near/far plan.
✓ Use color choices on purpose.
✓ Use light and dark value somewhere.
✓ Add at least two texture areas or marks.
📸

Photo reminder: You will upload a clear photo of your art or design product in Canvas. Make sure the whole page is visible.

1
✨ Revise One Part
Assessment work gets stronger when artists improve it.
10 min

Choose one area to improve so the depth is easier to see. What will you change?

Step 1: Hold your work away from your face.
Step 2: Compare it to the criteria.
Step 3: Improve one part. Do not restart.
Revision note: What did you improve?
2
📝 Artist Statement
Use simple art words and evidence.
10 min

My work shows depth because ____. I used ____ and ____ to help.

Write your artist statement.
