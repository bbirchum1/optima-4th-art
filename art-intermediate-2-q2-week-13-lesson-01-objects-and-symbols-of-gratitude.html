🎨 Art Words
Small words for clear art thinking.
10 min

Read the cards. Try to use one word in your answer today.

symbol

A symbol is a picture or object that stands for an idea. For example, a heart can stand for love, and a light bulb can stand for an idea.

still life

A still life is an artwork of objects that are not moving, such as fruit, flowers, cups, books, or tools.

gratitude

Gratitude means thankfulness. In art, a gratitude object can show something or someone you are thankful for.

arrangement

Arrangement means where things are placed. Artists arrange objects, shapes, colors, and empty spaces on purpose.
